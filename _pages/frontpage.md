Welcome to my research page! My name is Gage DeZoort and I'm an incoming postdoc in the Princeton University Physics and ORFE departments. I'm interested in developing principles for machine learning initialization / architecture selection and applying them to physics tasks.

My background is in high energy physics, specifically searches for new physics and applications of graph neural networks to charged particle tracking. I've also worked GNN principles in collaboration with my soon-to-be postdoc advisor, [Boris Hanin](https://boris-hanin.github.io/). You can view a preprint of our recent article here: [arxiv:2306.11668](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.11668). 

If any of this sounds interesting, I'd love to hear from you. Please feel free to contact me at jdezoort@princeton.edu.
