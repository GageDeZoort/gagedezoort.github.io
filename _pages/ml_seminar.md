The Princeton Physics and ORFE departments are hosting a *Machine Learning in Physics* seminar in the 2023-2024 academic year. The focus is on interdisciplinary work at the intersection of physics and ML, highlighting both ML with physics applications and physics aided by ML. All relevant information and resources will be posted to this page throughout the academic year. We're preparing a fantastic lineup of speakers, so stay tuned!
